U.S. Representative: District 6

Congressman Luke Messer served two terms in the Indiana State House before being elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2012. He graduated summa cum laude from Vanderbilt University. Messer was a former president and CEO of School Choice Indiana and Hoosiers for Economic Growth.

Indiana Secretary of State

Secretary of State Connie Lawson was first appointed by Gov. Mitch Daniels in 2012. She previously served as a state senator for 16 years and was the first woman to be the Majority Floor Leader in the Indiana Senate. A Danville native, Lawson served as the clerk of the Hendricks County Circuit Court for eight years.
