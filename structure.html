So, here's the scoop. The Unified Media Design Studio is a consists of a group of students from Ball State University dedicated to designing publications for print, phones, tablets and computers. In a world of ever-improving techonology, it is necessary for designers to improve, too. The work we do doesn't just sit on a platform. It pushes stories forward.

As a matter of fact, we designed this website. Need more proof? Take a look at some of the work we've done.










How do we function?

To support the design of each publication, we have a system that allows editors to specialize in specific areas while designers get exposure all around.

  1. Art director – oversees the studio
  2. Publication editors – oversee individual publications
  3. Lead designers – assist in training new members
  4. Specialized designers – focus on more challenging pieces
  5. Multi-platform designers – get experience immediately working for each publication

With this system in place, the studio listens to the concerns of the editorial staffs from each publication to ensure design helps move the stories.
